The Problem

As seen previously, the main problem identified by my dataset is the uneven spread of eligible bachelors in Sydney. These men are mainly located in Sydney's CBD, Lower North Shore, and Eastern Suburbs.

The Solution

To fix this problem, I want to create a large monorail system. The aim of this is to connect the north and south of Sydney and spread the men. Additionally, The monorail stations will have cafes, and recreational areas where single people can hang out and mingle.
