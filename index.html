Sydney's Most Eligible Bachelors

Educated Males

Using the 2016 ABS data set "Level of Education by Age by Sex," this map shows the proportion of males between the ages of 25-34 with a bachelor degree.

This image displays the proportion of males between the ages of 25 and 34 who have completed a bachelor degree. Unsurprisingly, there are large numbers of educated males near Macquarie University, Sydney University, The University of Technology Sydney, and The University of New South Wales. Further, there are large proportions of educated males in Paramatta, Rockdale, Wolli Creek, Mascot, Newtown, Surry Hills and the CBD. This may be due to the fact that there are more males of that age group living in those areas. The majority of these educated males are located in the South of Sydney and the latte line can be seen just above Mosman to Hornsby.


On the right, you can see the proportion of males between 25 and 34 with a bachelor degree and on the left you can see the proportion of males within the same age range in each area. As you can see, areas with high proportions of educated males also have high levels of males in the age range. Thus, those areas above the latte line are not necessarily lacking educated males, they are likely lacking young men.
